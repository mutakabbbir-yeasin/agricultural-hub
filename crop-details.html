Crop Details

Wheat (Grain Crop)

Varieties: Hard red wheat, Durum, Soft white.

Cultivation Methods: Sown in well-prepared soil using traditional or modern drilling methods.

Soil Requirements: Well-drained loamy soil with moderate fertility.

Harvesting: Harvested when grains turn golden, using combine harvesters.

Nutritional Value: High in carbohydrates, fiber, vitamins B-complex.

Uses: Bread, flour, pasta, bakery items.

Rice (Grain Crop)

Varieties: Basmati, Jasmine, Long-grain, Short-grain.

Cultivation Methods: Grown in flooded fields or paddies.

Soil Requirements: Clayey soil that retains water.

Harvesting: Harvested when grains mature and moisture reduces.

Nutritional Value: Rich in carbs, low fat, gluten-free.

Uses: Cooked meals, rice flour, rice bran oil.

Apple (Fruit Crop)

Varieties: Fuji, Gala, Honeycrisp, Red Delicious.

Cultivation Methods: Grown in temperate climates with pruning & grafting.

Soil Requirements: Well-drained loamy soil rich in organic matter.

Harvesting: Picked manually when firm and fully colored.

Nutritional Value: Fiber, vitamin C, antioxidants.

Uses: Juices, cider, fresh consumption, desserts.

Tomato (Vegetable Crop)

Varieties: Cherry, Roma, Beefsteak, Heirloom.

Cultivation Methods: Grown using seedlings with staking or trellising.

Soil Requirements: Well-drained, fertile soil rich in organic matter.

Harvesting: Picked when fruits turn red and firm.

Nutritional Value: Vitamin A, C, potassium, lycopene.

Uses: Sauce, salads, ketchup, cooking.

Cotton (Cash Crop)

Varieties: Upland cotton, Pima cotton, Egyptian cotton.

Cultivation Methods: Grown in warm climates; requires irrigation and timely pest control.

Soil Requirements: Deep, well-drained soil.

Harvesting: Mechanically picked when bolls open.

Sugarcane (Cash Crop)

Varieties: Co 86032, Co 0238, Saccharum hybrids.

Cultivation Methods: Grown from cuttings in tropical climates.

Soil Requirements: Fertile loamy or alluvial soil.

Harvesting: Cut manually or by machine when stems mature.

Nutritional Value: Rich in sucrose; used for juice extraction.

Uses: Sugar, jaggery, ethanol, biofuel.
